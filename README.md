
# Amazon Sales Dashboard 📊

## Overview

The **Amazon Sales Dashboard** is an interactive Power BI report designed to analyze Amazon product sales performance, discounts, ratings, reviews, users, and pricing trends.

It provides business insights to support decision-making in product strategy, pricing optimization, and category performance analysis.

---

## Objective

The goal of this dashboard is to:

* Monitor overall sales and discount performance
* Analyze product category distribution
* Track user engagement through reviews and ratings
* Compare actual vs discounted pricing
* Identify top-performing and low-performing product segments

---

## Key Metrics (KPIs)

* **Total Discount** – 698.68
* **Average Discount** – 0.48
* **Total Products** – 1,337
* **Total Reviews** – 1,212
* **Total Users** – 1,194
* **Average Rating** – 4.10
* **Total Rating Count** – 6.00K

---

## Dashboard Features

### 1. Pricing Analysis

* Min & Max actual price comparison
* Price trend visualization
* Discount impact analysis

### 2. Rating Analysis

* Min & Max rating count trends
* Overall rating distribution
* Customer satisfaction tracking

### 3. Category Analysis

* Category 1 & Category 2 breakdown
* Product distribution by categories
* Performance comparison across segments

### 4. Product-Level Insights

* Individual product filtering
* Review and rating comparison
* Discount analysis per product
